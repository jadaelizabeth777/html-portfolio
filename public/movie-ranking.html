My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Focus (Will Smith)

This movie is all about focus, Basically its a story about a con named Nico. Nico is a professional con man, and he walks you through his life. He meets a girl named Jess and everything changes...

Suicide Squad

This movie is based on DC characters and has two of my favorite actors from Focus in it. Its about a squad of gifted " Bad Guys" who are sent on suicide missions the military cant go on.

Spider-Man into the spider-verse

This movie is about a newer spider man, Miles Morales. This movie basically walks you through the beginning of his story.
